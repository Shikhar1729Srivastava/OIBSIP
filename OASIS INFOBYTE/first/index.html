Genius' Playground


Chess is a timeless game of strategy and intellect, played on a checkered board with 64 squares.
It's a battle of wits where two players maneuver their pieces strategically, aiming to outsmart and outmaneuver their opponent. With origins dating back over a millennium, chess has evolved into a global phenomenon, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and patience. From casual players to grandmasters, it offers endless possibilities for exploration and mastery.
Whether played for leisure or in competitive tournaments, chess remains a captivating pastime that transcends generations and cultures, inspiring admiration for its complexity and elegance.

Chess.com


The site has a freemium model in which some features are available for free, and others are available for accounts with subscriptions. Live online chess can be played against other users in daily, rapid, blitz or bullet time controls, with a number of chess variants available.



Lichess.com


Lichess is a free and open-source Internet chess server run by a non-profit organization of the same name. Users of the site can play online chess anonymously and optionally register an account to play rated games. Lichess is ad-free and all the features are available for free, as the site is funded by donations from patrons. Features include chess puzzles, computer analysis, tournaments and chess variants.
